Ameixa is a Material Design inspired icon theme aiming to provide a consistent and minimalistic look to your device. This theme supports mainly FLOSS apps icons, but privative app icons are included if useful. There is also a <a href="https://f-droid.org/en/packages/org.xphnx.ameixamonochrome/">black and white version available</a>.

Suported launchers:
<ul>
<li>Trebuchet</li>
<li>Kiss</li>
<li>Nova</li>
<li>Apex</li>
<li>Holo</li>
<li>Asus</li>
<li>Adw</li>
<li>OpenLauncher</li>
<li>...and any other launcher supporting icon themes</li>
</ul>
